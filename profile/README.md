## What We Build

Covalida is an **EU Compliance One-Stop-Shop** — a self-service portal that lets companies structure, document, and certify their compliance with EU regulation in one place, backed by accredited third-party certification bodies.

## EC-REP — EU Authorized Representative Service

Non-EU manufacturers placing regulated products on the EU single market require a **physically resident Authorized Representative (EC-REP)**. Covalida provides this as a fully managed service: structured onboarding, document custody, regulatory point-of-contact, and a verified address in Germany.

Particularly suited for manufacturers from China and Southeast Asia navigating **CRA, NIS2, PPWR, GSPR**, and **MDR**.

→ [Learn more at covalida.com/s/ec-rep](https://covalida.com/s/ec-rep)

## Compliance & Accreditation

Covalida operates as a **Certification Body (CB)** under the applicable ISO/IEC standards (17065, 17021, 17024) and as an EU Authorized Representative. All certification activities are subject to accreditation oversight.

For compliance or accreditation enquiries: [compliance@covalida.com](mailto:compliance@covalida.com)
